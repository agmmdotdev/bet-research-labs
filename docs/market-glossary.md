# Market Glossary

## Decimal odds

Decimal odds show total return including stake.

Example: 1 unit at 1.70 returns 1.70 units if it wins: 1 unit stake + 0.70 profit.

## Moneyline / match winner

A bet on a team to win in normal time, usually 90 minutes plus stoppage time. In knockout football, this is not the same as “to qualify.” A draw after 90 minutes loses a normal-time moneyline bet.

## To qualify

A bet on which team advances, including extra time and penalties if required. Usually safer than normal-time moneyline for favorites, but priced shorter.

## Both Teams To Score (BTTS)

A bet on whether both teams score at least one goal.

- **BTTS Yes**: both teams score.
- **BTTS No**: at least one team fails to score.

## Over / Under totals

A bet on total goals in the match.

Examples:

- Over 2.5 wins with 3 or more goals.
- Under 3.5 wins with 3 or fewer goals.

## Asian total

Asian totals split stakes across adjacent lines when the line is a quarter number.

Example: **Over 2.25** means half the stake on Over 2.0 and half on Over 2.5.

| Total goals | Over 2.25 result |
|---:|---:|
| 0 or 1 | Full loss |
| Exactly 2 | Half loss |
| 3+ | Full win |

## Asian handicap

Asian handicap adjusts the score before settlement and can remove or reduce draw outcomes.

Example: **Team -0.75** means half the stake on -0.5 and half on -1.0.

| Team result | -0.75 result |
|---|---:|
| Win by 2+ | Full win |
| Win by 1 | Half win |
| Draw or lose | Full loss |

Example: **Team -2.25** means half the stake on -2.0 and half on -2.5.

| Team result | -2.25 result |
|---|---:|
| Win by 3+ | Full win |
| Win by exactly 2 | Half loss |
| Win by 1, draw, or lose | Full loss |

## Double chance

A bet that covers two of the three normal-time outcomes.

- **1X**: home team win or draw.
- **X2**: away team win or draw.
- **12**: either team wins; draw loses.

Double chance is similar to +0.5 Asian Handicap for the selected side.

## Result + total

A combined market requiring both the match result and goal total condition to land.

Examples:

- Favorite win + Under 4.5.
- Germany win + Under 3.5.
- Canada win + Under 4.5.

This is useful when a favorite is likely to control the match but a blowout is not expected.

## Team total

A bet on how many goals one team scores, regardless of opponent goals.

Example: Argentina team total Over 2.5 wins if Argentina score 3 or more, even if the final is 3-1.

## Win to nil

A bet on a team to win while keeping a clean sheet.

Example: Spain win to nil wins with 1-0, 2-0, 3-0, etc. It loses if Spain concede or fail to win.
